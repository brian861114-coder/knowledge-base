## Why this map exists

Once the core structure-processing-properties logic is in place, the next serious step is to test it against modern materials problems that are chemically complex, interface-heavy, and often data-assisted.

## Map structure

[[Defect Chemistry and Point Defects]] and [[Phase Stability in Complex Alloys]] provide the base logic for compositionally rich or defect-sensitive systems. [[High-Entropy Alloys]] and [[Nanomaterials]] then show how complexity and scale change the usual intuition about diffusion, strengthening, and stability.

[[Magnetic Materials]], [[Dielectric Materials]], and [[Thermoelectric Materials]] are functional clusters where transport and field response matter as much as mechanical integrity. [[Computational Materials Design]] and [[Atom Probe Tomography]] provide modern design and evidence layers for navigating that complexity.
