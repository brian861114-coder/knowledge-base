## Why this map exists

Functional materials are often judged by their electronic, magnetic, or optical response, but those responses only make sense when tied back to structure, defects, and process history.

## Map structure

[[Electrical Properties]] is the broad entry point. [[Carrier Mobility]] and [[Dielectric Response]] split two major subcases of charge transport and polarization. [[Magnetic Response]] and [[Optical Properties]] extend the same logic into field and photon response.

[[Band Structure and Electronic States]] and [[Doping and Carrier Control]] are included because the property pages are not self-explanatory without the underlying electronic-structure logic.
