## Why this map exists

Property pages become useless if they remain a flat list. Engineers do not choose materials by reading isolated scalar values. They choose by understanding which property family matters, what controls it, and which tradeoffs come bundled with it.

## Map structure

[[Mechanical Properties Map]] covers deformation, strength, toughness, fatigue, creep, and wear. [[Thermal and Transport Properties Map]] covers heat flow, expansion, transition behavior, ionic transport, and coupled transport tradeoffs. [[Electronic Magnetic and Optical Properties Map]] covers electrical conduction, dielectric behavior, magnetic response, and optical behavior.

The point is not to worship property tables. The point is to understand how a measured property reflects structure, processing history, defects, and the environment in which the material must survive.
