## Why this map exists

Mechanical performance is where many materials arguments either become rigorous or fall apart. One strength number is never enough.

## Map structure

[[Elastic Modulus and Stiffness]] describes resistance to elastic deformation. [[Strength and Yield Behavior]] describes when permanent deformation begins or when load capacity peaks. [[Ductility and Plastic Flow]] asks how much irreversible deformation the material can accommodate before failure.

[[Fracture Toughness]], [[Fatigue Resistance]], and [[Creep Resistance]] cover three different ways a structure can lose integrity even when simple yield-based screening looks acceptable. [[Wear Resistance]] handles contact-driven surface durability.
