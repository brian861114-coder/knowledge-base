## Why this map exists

Processing is not a menu of factory operations. It is the controlled rewriting of structure through kinetics, transport, and stored energy. If that causal chain is missing, the knowledge base collapses into disconnected unit operations.

## Map structure

[[Nucleation and Growth]] and [[Solidification and Microsegregation]] explain how structure first appears or redistributes during thermal evolution. [[Recovery Recrystallization and Grain Growth]] explains how deformation-stored energy is reduced and how new grain structures emerge during annealing or thermomechanical routes.

[[Thin Film Growth and Residual Stress]] handles the special case where structure forms under strong interface, flux, and constraint effects. [[Casting and Solidification Processing]], [[Annealing]], [[Quenching and Tempering]], [[Sintering]], and [[Additive Manufacturing]] then provide the main engineering routes that exploit those mechanisms.
