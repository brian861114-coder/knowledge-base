## Why this map exists

The current knowledge base had semiconductor materials, but not the actual causal chain that makes semiconductors useful: carrier control, interfaces, films, and analysis.

## Map structure

[[Band Structure and Electronic States]] and [[Doping and Carrier Control]] define the logic of carrier behavior and defect-sensitive transport. [[Semiconductor Materials]] sits downstream of that logic.

[[Thin Film Deposition]] and [[Surface Engineering]] matter because semiconductor systems are built through layer control rather than bulk shaping. [[X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy]] and [[Raman Spectroscopy]] provide the surface and bonding evidence needed to interpret whether the material or interface is actually what you think it is.
