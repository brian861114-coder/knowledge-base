## Why this map exists

This is the central engineering map. If the knowledge base does not make this loop explicit, it will collapse into disconnected encyclopedia entries.

## Map structure

[[Diffusion in Solids]] and [[Phase Transformation Kinetics]] explain why thermal and chemical processing can change internal structure. [[Heat Treatment]] is the cleanest example because small changes in thermal history can move a material between very different microstructures.

[[Microstructure]] is the bridge note. It links process history to grain size, phase fractions, precipitates, porosity, and interfaces. Those in turn drive [[Mechanical Properties]], [[Electrical Properties]], and [[Thermal Properties]].

[[Thin Film Deposition]] and [[Additive Manufacturing]] show that the same loop operates in modern manufacturing, not just in classical bulk metallurgy.
