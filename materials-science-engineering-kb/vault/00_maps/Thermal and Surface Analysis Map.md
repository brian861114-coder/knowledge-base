## Why this map exists

Without thermal and surface analysis, this knowledge base still leans too hard toward structure imaging and not enough toward chemistry change, transitions, and degradation evidence.

## Map structure

[[Differential Scanning Calorimetry]] and [[Thermogravimetric Analysis]] capture thermal transitions, decomposition, and mass-change behavior. [[X-ray Photoelectron Spectroscopy]] and [[Raman Spectroscopy]] provide chemistry-sensitive surface and bonding information that complements diffraction and microscopy.

These methods matter directly for [[Polymer Processing]], [[Corrosion Resistance]], [[Oxidation Resistance]], and thin-film systems where surface state controls performance more than bulk averages do.
