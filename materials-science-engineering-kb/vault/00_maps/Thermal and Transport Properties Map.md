## Why this map exists

Heat and transport properties quietly dominate many design failures. Materials often fail not because they were too weak, but because they expanded differently, transported the wrong species too slowly, or degraded under thermal load.

## Map structure

[[Thermal Properties]] gives the broad cluster, while [[Thermal Expansion]] focuses on dimensional mismatch and thermal-stress consequences. [[Glass Transition and Thermal Transitions]] handles structurally important thermal events in amorphous or partially ordered systems.

[[Ionic Conductivity]] and [[Thermoelectric Transport Tradeoffs]] cover transport-limited functional behavior. [[Oxidation Resistance]] matters here because high-temperature service is a transport and chemical-stability problem as much as a mechanical one.
