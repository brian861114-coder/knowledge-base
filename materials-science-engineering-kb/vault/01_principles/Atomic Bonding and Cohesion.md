## Why it matters

Bonding is where material behavior starts. If you do not understand the bonding picture, every later claim about stiffness, brittleness, conductivity, or melting point is just pattern matching.

## Core relation

Bond type and bond strength shape equilibrium spacing, cohesive energy, and the cost of defect formation. Metallic, ionic, covalent, van der Waals, and mixed bonding each bias the range of structures and responses that become likely.
