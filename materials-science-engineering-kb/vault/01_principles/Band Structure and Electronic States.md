## Why it matters

For electronic materials, this is the deep structure behind every transport claim. If this layer is wrong, the rest is decorative nonsense.

## Core relation

Allowed and forbidden electronic states emerge from atomic arrangement, bonding, and periodic potential. Defects, disorder, and composition shifts can then alter carrier density, mobility, recombination, and leakage pathways.
