## Why it matters

In many functional materials, defect populations are not an imperfection layered on top of the physics. They are the physics.

## Core relation

Point defects change local composition, charge balance, diffusion pathways, and site occupancy. Their concentration is controlled by chemistry, temperature, external atmosphere, and coupled electrochemical conditions.

## Links

[[Ionic Conductivity]] often depends directly on defect-mediated hopping pathways. [[Solid Electrolytes]] and many oxides cannot be understood without a defect chemistry frame. [[Dielectric Materials]] are also defect-sensitive because leakage, polarization, and breakdown routes all depend on non-ideal structure.
