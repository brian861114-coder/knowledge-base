## Why it matters

Useful semiconductor behavior does not emerge by accident. Carrier populations have to be engineered, stabilized, and prevented from being ruined by defects or bad interfaces.

## Core relation

Dopants and compensating defects shift carrier concentration, activation energy, trap populations, and junction behavior. The intended effect depends on whether the dopant goes where you want, stays there, and avoids creating worse side effects.
