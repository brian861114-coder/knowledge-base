## Why it matters

Hydrogen can turn a respectable material into a brittle failure with almost no public warning in the usual property tables.

## Core relation

Hydrogen uptake, transport, trapping, and release depend on defects, interfaces, stress state, and environment. Once inside, hydrogen can alter local cohesion, plasticity, and crack growth behavior.

## Links

[[Hydrogen Embrittlement]] is the failure mode that makes this principle unavoidable. [[Environmental Assisted Cracking]] often overlaps because chemical and mechanical drivers rarely stay separate in service.
