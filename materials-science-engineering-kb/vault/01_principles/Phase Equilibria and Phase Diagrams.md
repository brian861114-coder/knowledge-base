## Why it matters

Phase diagrams are the engineer's compressed map of thermodynamic possibility. They do not tell you everything, but without them you are effectively guessing.

## Core relation

At equilibrium, the stable phase assemblage is the one that minimizes free energy subject to composition and external constraints. Phase diagrams project that information into a visual form that guides alloy design, ceramic processing, and heat-treatment planning.

## Links

[[Microstructure]] is shaped by which phases can coexist and in what fractions. [[Heat Treatment]] uses this knowledge to target dissolution, precipitation, or transformation windows. [[Material Classes]] often differ because different chemistries make different phase spaces accessible.
