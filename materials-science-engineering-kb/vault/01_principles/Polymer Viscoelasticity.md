## Why it matters

If you treat polymers like weakened metals, you will misread nearly everything that matters. Time and temperature are part of the constitutive law, not nuisance variables.

## Core relation

Chain mobility, entanglement, crystallinity, and temperature govern how deformation is stored, relaxed, or dissipated. The same polymer can look stiff, compliant, tough, or fragile depending on rate and thermal state.
