## Why it matters

Ceramics dominate where heat, wear, oxidation, or dielectric behavior matter more than ductility.

## Core idea

Ionic and covalent bonding often produce strong stiffness and high thermal stability, but limited plastic accommodation and strong sensitivity to flaws. Processing therefore becomes a major bottleneck.

## Links

[[Sintering]] is a core ceramic route because dense bulk parts often start from powders. [[Fracture Mechanisms]] matters because cracks and defects dominate ceramic reliability.
