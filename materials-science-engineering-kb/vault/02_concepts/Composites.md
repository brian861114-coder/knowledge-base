## Why it matters

Composites are one of the clearest demonstrations that structure at multiple scales can be engineered deliberately rather than accepted as given.

## Core idea

A composite derives its behavior from constituent choice, geometry, load transfer, and interface quality. The interface is usually where the promises and failures both concentrate.

## Links

[[Mechanical Properties]] become architecture-dependent rather than just composition-dependent. [[Corrosion]] and environment-sensitive degradation often attack matrix, reinforcement, or interfaces differently.
