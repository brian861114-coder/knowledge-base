## Why it matters

Dielectrics are where tiny structural defects can turn a useful insulator into a leaky, unstable failure.

## Core idea

Dielectric function depends on polarization mechanisms, defect content, interfaces, phase constitution, and field history. Thin films and ceramics are especially sensitive because processing can easily imprint harmful defects.
