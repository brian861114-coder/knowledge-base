## Why it matters

Magnetic materials show how small structural changes can radically alter functional behavior without obvious macroscopic geometry change.

## Core idea

Magnetic behavior depends on composition, crystal symmetry, defects, domain structure, and temperature. Useful performance is a microstructural engineering problem, not just a chemistry label.
