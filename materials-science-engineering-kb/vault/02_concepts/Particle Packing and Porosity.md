## Why it matters

Porosity is not just empty space. It is a structural feature that changes stress concentration, permeability, shrinkage path, and local transport.

## Core idea

Packing describes how particles arrange before full densification, while porosity describes the shape, size, connectivity, and distribution of the voids that remain. Open and closed porosity, as well as pore shape and clustering, can have very different consequences for later processing and final performance.

## Links

[[Sintering]] is fundamentally a process of reorganizing this pore network while increasing neck growth and density. [[Mechanical Properties]] and [[Corrosion]] often degrade sharply when pores remain connected or poorly distributed.
