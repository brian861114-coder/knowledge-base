## Why it matters

Polymers are indispensable where low density, flexible processing, insulation, and surface tailoring matter more than high-temperature strength.

## Core idea

Polymer behavior depends on chain mobility, crystallinity, crosslinking, and viscoelastic response. Time and temperature are not side notes here; they are part of the constitutive picture.
