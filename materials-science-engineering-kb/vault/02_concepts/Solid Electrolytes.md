## Why it matters

Solid electrolytes promise safety and architectural advantages, but they replace one set of liquid-electrolyte problems with a different set of interfacial and mechanical problems.

## Core idea

The material must support fast ion transport, resist decomposition, maintain useful contact, and survive chemo-mechanical stress at interfaces. That combination is hard because transport, stability, and processability usually pull in different directions.
