## Why it matters

High carrier density means little if carriers cannot move cleanly. Mobility is where crystal quality, scattering, and interface damage all cash out.

## Definition

Carrier mobility captures the drift response of electrons or holes under electric field. It is a transport-quality metric, not just a composition label.

## What controls it

Scattering from phonons, defects, impurities, interfaces, grain boundaries, and strain all matter. Process changes that improve one electronic metric can easily damage mobility by increasing disorder or trap density.
