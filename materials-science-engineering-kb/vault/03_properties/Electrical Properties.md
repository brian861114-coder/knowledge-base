## Why it matters

Electrical behavior is decisive in electronics, energy storage, sensing, and many coatings. It is also a fast way to expose whether a structural change actually altered functionality.

## Definition

This property cluster includes conductivity, resistivity, carrier mobility, dielectric response, and related transport behavior. The exact metric depends on whether the material is metallic, semiconducting, ionic, or insulating.

## What controls it

Bonding, band structure, defects, interfaces, composition gradients, and microstructure all matter. Thin films are especially sensitive because residual stress, texture, and interface quality can distort transport severely.
