## Why it matters

For polymers and many amorphous systems, transition temperatures are not trivia. They define whether the material is usable, processable, or already in the wrong mechanical regime.

## Definition

This cluster covers the glass transition, melting, crystallization, and related thermal events that mark changes in mobility or phase state.

## What controls it

Chain architecture, plasticization, crystallinity, thermal history, and composition all matter. A material can cross from stable to useless by a modest shift around one transition region.
