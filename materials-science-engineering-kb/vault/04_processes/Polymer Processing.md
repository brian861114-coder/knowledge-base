## Why it matters

For polymers, processing is inseparable from property formation. Shape-making and structure-making happen at the same time.

## Workflow

Typical routes include extrusion, molding, curing, and thermal consolidation. Each route balances viscosity control, cooling rate, flow-induced orientation, and dimensional stability.

## Structure impact

The process changes crystallinity, chain orientation, void content, weld-line quality, and residual stress. Those changes feed directly into [[Viscoelastic Response]], environmental durability, and surface behavior.
