## Why it matters

Many failures and functional responses start at the surface, not in the bulk. Surface engineering exists because the bulk rarely gives you everything you need.

## Workflow

Routes include coating, texturing, chemical conversion, implantation, polishing, and other surface-specific treatments. The exact choice depends on whether the goal is protection, adhesion, transport control, or biological response.

## Structure impact

Surface engineering changes roughness, chemistry, residual stress, film continuity, and interface quality. Those changes strongly influence [[Corrosion Resistance]], adhesion, and contact-driven behavior.
