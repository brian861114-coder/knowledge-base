## Why it matters

Bulk mechanical tests are often too blunt for coatings, gradients, and thin modified surface regions. Nanoindentation exists because the important mechanics can be local.

## What it measures

The method records force-displacement response during controlled indentation, enabling estimation of local hardness, modulus, and sometimes creep-like or rate-sensitive behavior.

## Interpretation

Surface roughness, substrate influence, indentation size, and time dependence all matter. If those are ignored, the numbers become decorative rather than informative.
