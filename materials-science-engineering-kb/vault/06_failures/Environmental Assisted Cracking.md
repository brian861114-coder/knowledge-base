## Why it matters

This failure mode is where naive separation of mechanics and chemistry falls apart. A material can survive the stress alone and the environment alone, then fail rapidly under both together.

## Core idea

Chemical reaction, hydrogen uptake, localized dissolution, or film rupture can reduce crack resistance or accelerate crack advance under load. The exact mechanism depends on material family and environment.
