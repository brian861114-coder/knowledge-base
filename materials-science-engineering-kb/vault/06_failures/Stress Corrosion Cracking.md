## Why it matters

Stress corrosion cracking is one of the classic reasons a material that looks acceptable in air fails disastrously in service. The environment is not background noise here. It is part of the failure mechanism.

## Core idea

Cracks initiate and propagate because stress and environment cooperate. Local dissolution, film rupture, embrittlement, and crack-tip chemistry can all contribute. The key point is specificity: many materials are vulnerable only in particular environments and loading conditions.
