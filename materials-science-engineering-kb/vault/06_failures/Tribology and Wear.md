## Why it matters

Wear failures are usually cumulative, dirty, and easy to underestimate until the interface is already destroyed.

## Core idea

Wear emerges from repeated contact, adhesion, abrasion, surface fatigue, tribochemical reaction, and debris evolution. The dominant mechanism depends on load, counterface, lubrication, and environment.

## Links

[[Wear Resistance]] is the property summary, but [[Surface Engineering]] and [[Coatings and Protective Layers]] are the main intervention routes. In biomaterials, wear debris can create both mechanical and biological failure pathways.
