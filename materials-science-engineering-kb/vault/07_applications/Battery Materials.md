## Why it matters

Battery systems force materials science to deal with coupled failure modes instead of isolated property targets. That makes them intellectually honest and technically brutal.

## Core idea

Electrodes, electrolytes, current collectors, and interfaces must all survive transport, redox cycling, thermal load, and mechanical evolution. Performance gains usually create new instability pathways somewhere else.

## Links
