## Why it matters

Cathodes often define energy density, cost, and degradation pathways all at once. They are not just one component among equals.

## Core idea

The host structure must reversibly accept and release ions while preserving enough structural and chemical stability to survive many cycles. High energy and long life usually fight each other directly.

## Links

[[Electrochemical Stability]] matters at both bulk and interface levels. [[Corrosion]] appears here as parasitic surface or interface degradation rather than only as classical structural corrosion.
