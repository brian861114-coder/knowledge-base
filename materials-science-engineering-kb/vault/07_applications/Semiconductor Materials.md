## Why it matters

Semiconductors show how brutally sensitive functional materials can be to defects, surfaces, and process history. This is materials science under tight tolerance.

## Core idea

Useful semiconductor behavior depends on band structure, doping, defect control, interface quality, and the disciplined construction of thin layers and patterned regions.

## Links

[[Thin Film Deposition]] is central because many semiconductor devices are layer-built systems. [[Crystal Defects]] matter because tiny defect populations can dominate transport, recombination, or leakage behavior.
